# How to Use MBIS

A guide for legislators, journalists, students, and citizens.

---

## Quick start

Open the dashboard. Three things will be visible immediately:

1. **The headline number** — Maryland's FY2027 proposed operating budget ($70.8B)
2. **Six KPI cards** — Key fiscal indicators colored red, amber, or green
3. **A scrolling ticker** — 10 rotating headlines from the FY2027 budget

Scroll down to see the 10 visualizations and the county map.

---

## Reading the KPI cards

Each card has four parts:

- **Label** (e.g. "Structural Deficit Ahead")
- **Big number** (e.g. "$2.3B")
- **One-line context** (e.g. "Projected for next year's budget")
- **Citation** (e.g. "Board of Revenue Estimates")

The card's top border color indicates direction:

- **Purple** — informational (the headline budget number)
- **Green** — on track or improving
- **Amber** — caution; watch this
- **Red** — getting worse; needs attention

Hover any card for an entry animation and shadow lift.

---

## Reading the carousel (10 visualizations)

The carousel scrolls horizontally. You can:

- **Drag** the slide strip with your mouse
- **Use the arrow keys** ← →
- **Click the dots** at the top
- **Click the numbered tabs** at the bottom

Each slide has three parts:

1. **The chart itself** (interactive — click, hover, filter)
2. **A commentary sidebar** with the insight
3. **A source line** at the bottom of the sidebar with the exact PDF page

If you want to verify a number, click the source — it deep-links to the source PDF at the page.

---

## Reading the county map

The map shows Maryland's 24 counties (incl. Baltimore City). You can:

- **Switch metrics** — toggle between Budget, Poverty, Income, Population
- **Pick a year** — slider goes FY2015 to FY2026
- **Filter by agency** — dropdown narrows to a specific state agency
- **Hover a county** — see the metric value
- **Click a county** — drill into the county profile
- **Fullscreen** — click the ⛶ icon top-right
- **Reset zoom** — double-click anywhere on the map

## Common tasks

### "I want to see how Maryland's budget grew over 8 years."
→ Slide 1 of the carousel ("Maryland's Fiscal Journey"). Filter by year.

### "I want to see where the budget money flows."
→ Slide 2 ("Budget Flow — Revenue to Agency"). The Sankey diagram shows revenue → fund type → agency.

### "I want to know if my county got more or less money this year."
→ The county map. Pick "Budget" as the metric and use the year slider.

### "I want to know how a federal cut would hit Maryland."
→ Slide 7 ("Federal Cut Calculator"). Move the slider 0–20%.

### "I want to know what happens to Maryland education spending."
→ Slide 6 ("Blueprint"). Shows the legal mandate vs. the actual budget trajectory.

### "I want to verify a number."
→ Click the source citation under the chart. It opens the source PDF at the exact page.

---

## What MBIS does **not** answer

- "Should this agency be cut?"
- "Who is responsible for this deficit?"
- "Will the budget pass?"
- "What will the FY2031 budget look like?"

These are political and forecasting questions outside the dashboard's scope. See the Guardrails page for the full list.
